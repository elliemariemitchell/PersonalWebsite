About Me

Hi! Apart from my passion for political science, which was highlighted in my resume, I have many other interests and passions! This page is dedicated to some of those interests:

The Mountains

When I'm in Utah, I feel happiest when I’m in the mountains. I love hiking and camping in the summer and skiing in the winter. I have a ski pass at Sundance, the local resort that's closest to BYU, and I make it a priority during ski season to go up at least once or twice each week.

Travel

I love traveling more than anything else in the world. My family moved to Japan when I was in high school, sparking my love for exploring new places. Since then, I’ve prioritized traveling, studying abroad in London in 2022 and interning in Edinburgh in 2023. So far, I’ve visited 20 countries and already have plans to visit more!
